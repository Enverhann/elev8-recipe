Hamburger

Home

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced cheese (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, chopped onion, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine all the ingredients.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal parts and shape each part into a patty, about 1/2-inch thick.
  3. Heat a grill or a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook the burger patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
  4. If using cheese, place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to let it melt.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster until lightly browned.
  6. Assemble the burgers: Place a burger patty on the bottom half of each bun. Add lettuce, sliced tomatoes, and onions on top of the patty.
  7. Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top half of the buns (optional). Cover the burger with the top half of the bun.
  8. Serve the delicious hamburgers with your favorite side dishes.
