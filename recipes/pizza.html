Pizza

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings of your choice:
  • - Pepperoni slices
  • - Sliced bell peppers
  • - Sliced mushrooms
  • - Sliced onions
  • - Cooked and crumbled sausage
  • - Sliced olives
  • - Fresh basil leaves
  • - Grated Parmesan cheese
  • - Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 475°F or 245°C).
  2. If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  3. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  4. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  6. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  7. Add your preferred toppings, arranging them evenly over the cheese.
  8. If using a pizza stone, carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated stone in the oven.
  9. If using a baking sheet, simply place the baking sheet in the oven.
  10. Bake the pizza for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  11. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute.
  12. Sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese and red pepper flakes (if desired) over the pizza.
  13. Slice and serve the delicious homemade Pizza hot!
