Korean Toast

Home

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • Butter for spreading

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs with milk until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a little butter.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides.
  4. Place the dipped bread slices in the pan and cook until they are golden brown on both sides.
  5. Remove the toasted bread from the pan and set them aside.
  6. In the same pan, cook the ham slices until they are slightly crispy.
  7. Spread mayonnaise on one side of each toasted bread slice.
  8. On half of the bread slices (with mayo side up), place a slice of cheese and a slice of ham.
  9. Drizzle ketchup on top of the ham and cover with the remaining toasted bread slices (with mayo side down).
  10. Press the sandwiches gently to stick together.
  11. Cut the sandwiches in half diagonally and serve hot.
