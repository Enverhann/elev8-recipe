Baklava

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 package of phyllo dough
  • 2 cups of chopped nuts (walnuts, pistachios, or almonds)
  • 1 cup of unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine the chopped nuts and ground cinnamon.
  3. Brush a baking dish with melted butter.
  4. Layer about 10 sheets of phyllo dough in the dish, brushing each layer with melted butter.
  5. Add a layer of the nut mixture on top of the phyllo dough.
  6. Continue layering phyllo dough and nuts until you run out of both, finishing with about 10 sheets of phyllo dough on top.
  7. Using a sharp knife, cut the baklava into diamond or square shapes.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes or until golden brown and crisp.
  9. While the baklava is baking, make the syrup by combining the sugar, water, honey, and vanilla extract in a saucepan. Bring to a boil and simmer for about 10 minutes until slightly thickened.
  10. Remove the baklava from the oven and immediately pour the hot syrup over it, making sure to cover all the pieces.
  11. Allow the baklava to cool completely before serving.
