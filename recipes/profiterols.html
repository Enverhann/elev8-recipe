Profiteroles

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup chocolate sauce or melted chocolate

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a saucepan, bring water, butter, and salt to a boil over medium-high heat.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and add all-purpose flour. Stir continuously until the mixture forms a ball and pulls away from the sides of the saucepan.
  4. Remove the saucepan from heat and let it cool for a couple of minutes.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition, until the dough is smooth and shiny.
  6. Transfer the dough to a piping bag fitted with a large round tip.
  7. Pipe small mounds of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, leaving space between each puff.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the profiteroles are golden and puffed.
  9. Remove the profiteroles from the oven and let them cool completely.
  10. In a mixing bowl, whip the heavy cream with powdered sugar and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  11. Cut the profiteroles in half horizontally and fill each with a dollop of whipped cream.
  12. Drizzle chocolate sauce or melted chocolate over the filled profiteroles.
  13. Serve the delicious Profiteroles immediately or refrigerate for later enjoyment.
