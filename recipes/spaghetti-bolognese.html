Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

Home

Ingredients

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for serving)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until softened and fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Break it apart with a spatula as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the canned diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, and dried basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens and the flavors meld together.
  6. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
